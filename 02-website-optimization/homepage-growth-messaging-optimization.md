# Homepage Growth Messaging Optimization

## Executive Summary

This project focused on repositioning the Potere Search homepage to better communicate the company's value as a strategic recruiting partner that helps organizations accomplish meaningful business goals through high-impact hiring.

The messaging was updated to shift the focus away from recruiting processes and toward business outcomes, company growth, and the long-term impact of hiring the right people.

---

## Business Objective

Support Potere Search's positioning as a growth-focused recruiting partner by improving the clarity, consistency, and effectiveness of the homepage messaging.

---

## Background

The original homepage emphasized recruiting services and hiring processes. Through leadership discussions and ongoing refinement, the messaging evolved to better reflect the company's philosophy of helping organizations achieve ambitious business goals through strategic hiring.

---

## Homepage Changes

### Main Headline

* Replaced the original homepage headline.
* Updated the supporting message to focus on business vision, growth, and meaningful outcomes.

### Business Value Section

Updated the three value sections to communicate a business-first approach:

* Define the BHAG
* Find the Best People
* Create Lasting Impact

The messaging now focuses on business objectives and measurable impact rather than recruiting activities.

### Potere Search Growth Model

Replaced the previous recruiting model messaging with a company-branded growth model centered on business goals, strategic hiring, and organizational impact.

### Leadership Placements

Updated the section title and supporting copy to better emphasize successful placements and business results.

### Executive Growth Insights

Updated article titles and supporting content to better align with Potere Search's positioning and target audience.

### Contact Section

Updated the contact section headline to "What's Your Big Goal?" to encourage conversations centered around business objectives rather than open positions.

---

## Leadership Feedback

The homepage was refined through multiple review cycles based on leadership feedback.

Key areas of focus included:

* Stronger business-oriented messaging.
* Reduced emphasis on recruiting processes.
* Greater focus on company growth and measurable impact.
* Consistent positioning across all homepage sections.
* Simpler and clearer messaging throughout the page.

---

## Outcome

The homepage now presents Potere Search as a strategic recruiting partner that helps organizations accomplish meaningful business goals by connecting them with high-impact people.

---

## Future Considerations

Future homepage improvements should continue strengthening the company's differentiation while maintaining clear, outcome-focused messaging that speaks directly to business leaders and hiring decision-makers.
