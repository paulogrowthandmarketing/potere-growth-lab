# Homepage Growth Messaging Optimization

## Objective

Align the Potere Search homepage with the company's business strategy by shifting the messaging from recruiting services to business outcomes, company growth, and the impact of hiring the right people.

---

## Background

The original homepage focused primarily on recruiting services and the hiring process.

Following internal discussions and leadership feedback, the homepage was updated to better communicate Potere Search's core value: helping companies accomplish ambitious business goals through strategic hiring.

---

## Goals

- Position Potere Search as a growth partner rather than a traditional recruiting firm.
- Focus on business outcomes instead of recruiting activities.
- Strengthen the overall value proposition.
- Improve consistency across the homepage.
- Create messaging that speaks directly to founders, CEOs, and hiring leaders.

---

## Homepage Updates

### Main Headline

Updated the homepage headline and supporting message to focus on business vision, ambitious goals, and the people needed to achieve them.

---

### Business Value Section

Replaced process-focused messaging with business-focused messaging centered on:

- Defining the business objective
- Finding the best people
- Creating measurable business impact

---

### Potere Search Growth Model

Replaced the previous recruiting model messaging with a framework centered on business goals, business impact, and hiring for meaningful outcomes.

---

### Leadership Placements

Updated the section title and messaging to better highlight successful placements and measurable business results.

---

### Contact Section

Updated the contact form headline to focus on the visitor's business goals rather than a generic contact request.

---

## Outcome

The homepage now presents Potere Search as a strategic recruiting partner focused on helping companies accomplish meaningful business goals through high-impact hiring rather than simply filling open positions.
