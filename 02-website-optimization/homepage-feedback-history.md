# Homepage Feedback History

## Purpose

This document records key feedback received throughout the homepage optimization project. It provides context for major messaging decisions and explains how the homepage evolved during the review process.

---

## Initial Direction

The homepage was originally revised to strengthen Potere Search's positioning around growth, leadership, and business outcomes while reducing emphasis on traditional recruiting language.

---

## Leadership Feedback

### Homepage Messaging

The homepage should focus on helping companies accomplish meaningful business goals rather than simply filling positions.

The messaging should communicate that clients bring the vision while Potere Search helps them build the team needed to achieve it.

---

### Business Goals

The website should consistently reinforce that every search begins with understanding the client's business objective before identifying the right people.

---

### Growth Model

The previous "New Era Recruiting Model" created unnecessary confusion.

The messaging was updated to keep the focus on Potere Search rather than introducing a separate brand.

---

### Business Impact

Success should not be presented as filling open positions.

Success should be presented as the long-term business impact created by the people hired.

---

### High-Impact Hiring

Avoid positioning Potere Search as only serving executive leadership roles.

The focus should remain on identifying the people who create the greatest impact on a company's goals, regardless of title or level within the organization.

---

## Current Direction

The homepage now centers on four core principles:

- Business goals first
- High-impact hiring
- Measurable business outcomes
- Long-term company growth

---

## Status

Current and active.
