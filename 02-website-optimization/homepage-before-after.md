# Homepage Before & After

## Purpose

This document summarizes the approved homepage improvements completed for the Potere Search website.

---

## Objective

Improve the homepage messaging, branding, and user experience to better communicate Potere Search's value proposition and align with leadership feedback.

---

## Key Improvements

### Homepage Messaging

- Refined the homepage messaging to focus on business outcomes rather than simply filling positions.
- Improved the overall flow of information from the main message through the supporting sections.

---

### Potere Search Growth Model

Updated the Growth Model section to better communicate Potere Search's process.

Changes included:

- Stronger messaging
- Clearer business focus
- More outcome-driven language

---

### Contact Section

Updated the contact section heading to better align with Potere Search's messaging.

---

### Website Branding

Applied the updated Potere Search logo across the website.

---

## Leadership Feedback

The homepage was revised using feedback provided by Nick Morrison.

Major themes included:

- Focus more on business outcomes.
- Make messaging more tangible.
- Simplify wording.
- Better communicate Potere Search's approach.
- Improve overall clarity.
