# Homepage Audit

Date: June 2026

## Observation 1 - Hero Messaging

Current Headline:

"Scaling a Business is Hard. Hiring Should Be Easy."

### Analysis

The message positions Potere Search primarily as a recruiting firm.

Leadership direction is to position Potere Search as a strategic growth partner that helps businesses scale through talent acquisition, executive search, and organizational growth.

### Recommendation

Future homepage messaging should emphasize:

- Business growth
- Organizational scaling
- Leadership hiring
- Strategic talent acquisition
- Long-term partnerships

rather than focusing solely on recruiting services.
