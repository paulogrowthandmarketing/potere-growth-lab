# Week 01

## Role

Head of Growth & Marketing

## Primary Focus

Conduct an initial audit of growth systems, marketing infrastructure, website architecture, ATS implementation, and operational processes to identify opportunities for optimization and future growth initiatives.

## Activities Completed

### Website Optimization

* Accessed WordPress administration
* Audited website structure and navigation
* Reviewed Elementor implementation
* Evaluated user experience and conversion opportunities
* Identified areas for improving employer acquisition pathways
* Reviewed Job Postings page implementation

### ATS & Systems

* Investigated RecruiterFlow integration
* Located iframe implementation on the Job Postings page
* Accessed and reviewed Loxo Careers Pages
* Evaluated ATS migration requirements
* Documented publishing controls for confidential searches
* Assessed website-to-ATS integration workflow

### Growth Operations

* Reviewed existing marketing and operational systems
* Identified opportunities for process improvement
* Began documenting growth initiatives and workflows
* Established a framework for tracking growth-related projects
* Evaluated current infrastructure supporting employer acquisition

## Key Discoveries

* Existing ATS integration is connected through a RecruiterFlow iframe embedded within Elementor
* Loxo Careers Pages provides publishing controls that support confidential search requirements
* Website conversion opportunities exist across multiple user journeys
* Lead capture and employer acquisition pathways can be strengthened
* Existing systems require further documentation and optimization
* Growth initiatives will benefit from centralized reporting and tracking
